MeterOnAt / MeterOffAt (ISO 8601 UTC, taxi only) — the meter-running window. For taxi this is usually the same as pickup/dropoff, but during disputes the meter timestamps are the source of truth for fare calculation.
Service-type-specific extras (optional but very useful for disputes):
Food: OrderReadyAt (restaurant handover), DeliveryAt (drop-off at customer).
