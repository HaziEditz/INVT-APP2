Gap the architect caught — multi-queue visibility on dispatch

The driver-app dev wrote food/freight queues to driverQueue/{cid}/{did}/queuedMulti/{bookingId} in Firebase. But our dispatch console builds $scope.queuedJobs from the [GetQueuedJobs] poll, which reads our server's jobStore — not Firebase. So unless the driver app also calls our [QueueJob] REST endpoint for each multi-queue job, those jobs will be invisible in the dispatch UI: no amber Pre-Queue strip, no +N badge, no row in the queued panel.

The Firebase listener at driverQueue/{cid} does fire on queuedMulti writes, but all it does is trigger a [GetQueuedJobs] refresh — which still reads jobStore, which still doesn't know about those jobs.
